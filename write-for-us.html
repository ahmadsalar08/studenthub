Guidelines

How should you write?

Original content
The article must be written by you. Copy-pasted or AI-generated content will not be accepted.
Length
Minimum 400 words. Enough for the reader to learn something — avoid unnecessary padding.
Language
English is preferred. Write clearly so readers can easily understand.

Not just theory — readers should leave with something actionable. Real examples and tips are a must.
