What data do we collect?

When you use StudentHub, we collect some basic information:

  • Name & Email — when creating an account or on the contact form
  • Reading history — which articles were read (to improve the site)
  • Device & Browser info — to resolve technical issues
  • Location (approximate) — country level only, not exact

💡 We will never ask for sensitive data — password hints, financial info, or government IDs.

⚙️

How is this data used?

Your data is only used to improve the website:

  • Based on your preferences to suggest articles
  • To improve website performance
  • To send email for important updates or replies
  • To protect the community from spam

We do not sell your data to advertisers or any third party.

🍪

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files saved in your browser. We only use essential cookies:

  • Session cookies — to keep you logged in across page reloads
  • Preference cookies — to remember dark mode or settings preferences

We do not use any marketing or tracking cookies. You can clear them anytime from browser settings.

🤝

Who is data shared with?

Your personal data is not shared with anyone. The only exceptions are:

  • Supabase — for encrypted database storage only
  • Vercel — required for website hosting
  • Legal requirement — if officially required by a court

🔒 These services are only given the minimum necessary data, in encrypted form.

✅

What are your rights?

You can take these actions regarding your data:

  • View — see what data we have saved
  • Edit — correct any wrong information
  • Delete — permanently remove your account and all data
  • Object — refuse any specific use of your data

Use the contact form for any request. You will receive a response within 7 days.

🛡️

How secure is the data?

  • All data is transferred with HTTPS (SSL) encryption
  • Database protected with Supabase Row Level Security
  • Passwords are hashed — never stored as plain text
  • Regular security checks are performed

In case of a security breach, we will immediately email all affected users.

Any questions? 💬

If you have any concerns about privacy, feel free to contact us directly.
