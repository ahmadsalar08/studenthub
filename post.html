1st year CS student survival guide — jo koi nahi batata

University ka pehla saal bohot overwhelming lagta hai. Yahan woh sab jo mujhe pehle se pata hota toh meri life kitni aasan hoti.

AK
Ali Khan
CS Student · 3rd Semester · Lahore

Pehle din jab main university gaya, toh mujhe laga sab kuch samajh aa jayega. Lekin 2 hafte baad main completely lost tha. Assignments, labs, quizzes — sab ek saath. Yeh guide un sab students ke liye hai jo abhi abhi start kar rahe hain.

1. Pehle semester mein CGPA se zyada important cheez

Sab log CGPA ki baat karte hain lekin koi nahi batata ke pehle semester mein sabse important cheez hai routine banana. University school jaisi nahi hoti — yahan koi tumhara haath nahi pakdega. Khud se discipline maintain karni padti hai.

Mera suggestion hai ke pehle 4 hafton mein sirf ek kaam karo — har roz same time par class attend karo aur notes likho. Bas itna kaafi hai shuru mein.

Pro tip: Pehle semester mein 3.0+ CGPA maintain karna hi goal rakho. Baad mein improve karna relatively aasaan hota hai.

2. Dosto ka selection — sabse bada decision

University mein jo log tumhare around hote hain woh tumhe directly affect karte hain. Aise logon ke saath time spend karo jo:

  • Classes seriously lete hain
  • Naye cheezein seekhne mein interest rakhte hain
  • Tumhari progress mein genuinely khush hote hain
  • Problems share karte hain solutions ke saath

Yeh sunne mein boring lagta hai lekin trust me — 2 saal baad tum apne dosto ki average jaisi life jiyo ge.

3. Programming — sahi tareeqa kya hai?

CS students ka sabse common mistake yeh hai ke woh sirf lectures sun te hain aur notes likhte hain. Programming ek practical skill hai — isko sirf padh ke nahi seekha ja sakta.

Har din kam se kam 30 minute code likho. Chahe simple calculator ho, chahe basic calculator — kuch bhi. Hands-on practice theory se 10 guna zyada valuable hai.

Common mistake: YouTube tutorials dekh ke sochna ke programming aa gayi. Jab tak khud se problem solve nahi karo, kuch nahi seekha.

4. Resources jo actually kaam karte hain

Yeh free resources hain jo mujhe pehle semester se hi pata honi chahiye thi:

  • GeeksforGeeks — DSA aur programming concepts ke liye
  • CS50 Harvard — Best free CS course online
  • GitHub Student Pack — Hundreds of free tools
  • Notion — Notes organize karne ke liye
  • VS Code — Best code editor, completely free

5. Exams ki tayari — honest advice

Raat bhar jaagna aur last minute padhai — yeh kaam nahi karta. Main 3 semesters baad yeh bol raha hoon experience se. Jo strategy actually kaam karti hai:

  • Har week ke end mein us week ka revision karo (20-30 min)
  • Past papers zaroor solve karo — pattern samajh aata hai
  • Group study sirf un ke saath karo jo serious hain
  • Exam se 2 din pehle naya topic start mat karo
Yaad rakho: University sirf degree nahi deta — yeh seekhne ka time hai. Agar sahi use karo toh yeh 4 saal tumhari poori zindagi badal sakte hain.

Conclusion

1st year mushkil hoga — yeh normal hai. Har successful CS student is phase se guzra hai. Fark sirf itna hai ke unhon ne give up nahi kiya. Consistent raho, sahi logon ke saath time spend karo, aur har roz kuch seekho — baaki sab khud ho jayega.
