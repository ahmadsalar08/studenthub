Mission

Kyun banaya StudentHub?

2nd year mein jab main university mein struggle kar raha tha, toh mujhe aise resources nahi mile jo actually kaam karte. Jo bhi tha woh ya toh English mein tha ya phir Pakistani universities se related nahi tha.

StudentHub isliye banaya taake jo cheezein mujhe kisi ne nahi batai, woh main doosron ko bata sakoon. Real problems, real solutions — Urdu/Roman Urdu mein.

Practical advice
Sirf woh tips jo real zindagi mein actually kaam karte hain. Koi motivational fluff nahi.
Honest experience
Apni real struggles aur failures bhi share karta hoon — sirf success stories nahi.
Community
Ek jagah jahan Pakistani CS students apni problems discuss kar sakein aur ek doosre ki help karein.

Values

Jo cheezein important hain

01
Honesty pehle
Sirf woh likhunga jo actually sahi hai — koi fake positivity, koi exaggeration nahi. Agar kuch kaam nahi karta, woh bhi bataunga.
02
Pakistan ke context mein
Yahan jo advice milegi woh Pakistani universities, culture aur resources ko dhyan mein rakh ke hogi — foreign blogs ki copy nahi.
03
Student-friendly language
Roman Urdu mein likha jaata hai taake sab easily samajh sakein. Koi fancy English, koi jargon nahi.
04
Community first
Yeh sirf mera blog nahi — yeh tumhara bhi hai. Comments mein apne thoughts share karo, suggestions do, articles submit karo.
