Mission

Why StudentHub was created

In my 2nd year, when I was struggling at university, I couldn't find resources that actually worked. Everything was either in English or not relevant to Pakistani universities.

StudentHub was created so that the things nobody told me, I could tell others. Real problems, real solutions — in a language students here actually speak.

Practical advice
Only tips that actually work in real life. No motivational fluff.
Honest experience
Real struggles and failures are shared too — not just success stories.
Community
A place where Pakistani CS students can discuss problems and help each other.

Values

What matters here

01
Honesty first
Only what is actually true will be written here — no fake positivity, no exaggeration. If something doesn't work, that will be shared too.
02
Pakistani context
The advice here is written with Pakistani universities, culture, and resources in mind — not copied from foreign blogs.
03
Student-friendly language
Written in simple, clear English so everyone can understand. No fancy jargon.
04
Community first
This isn't just my blog — it's yours too. Share your thoughts in the comments, give suggestions, submit articles.
